Did You Know?

Lake Tahoe is the largest alpine lake in North America, sitting at 6,229 feet elevation on the California-Nevada border. Its crystal-clear water has a visibility of up to 70 feet and a stunning deep blue color. The lake holds enough water to cover the entire state of California to a depth of 14.5 inches.
